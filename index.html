About Me

Hi! I'm Jacob. That's me up there at the top of your screen. I'm a customer experience professional turned web developer and I have a passion for building websites and apps that provide an amazing user experience for everyone: your clients, your employees, and you.


Having worked in customer service for over 5 years, I keep a critical eye for usability from the customer perspective. I want clients to have as smooth experience as possible and get the info they need. I also have an eye for employee usability and I want everyone's experience to be enjoyable, for everyone to say "Wow, that was easy to use."


My top tools to get the job done include TypeScript, React, NextJS, SQL, MongoDB, Node, and Express.


Feel free to browse some of my past projects below and drop me a line if you think I'm a good fit for you. I look forward to working with you to create something big!
